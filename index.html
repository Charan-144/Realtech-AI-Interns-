About RealTech Interns

Nowadays, students experience only the theoretical part of engineering without enough real-world exposure. Through Reverse Experience Advanced Learning, we provide hands-on, practical guidance by Full Stack Leads and industry experts. Our goal is to make students understand both fundamentals (for 1st-year students) and advanced concepts (for 3rd & 4th-year students), preparing them to excel in the real tech world.

  • Daily assignments and weekly assessments.
  • Live classes from 6:30 PM to 7:30 PM; optional weekend on-campus practice.
